About SMCFI

Over three decades of empowering lives through education

Who We Are

The Simbayanan ni Maria Community Foundation, Inc. (SMCFI) is a non-stock, non-profit, and PCNC-accredited organization dedicated to providing educational opportunities to underprivileged yet deserving youth in Taguig City.

Since its establishment in 1991, SMCFI has focused on developing not only academically capable students but also socially responsible and values-driven individuals.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To provide access to quality education and holistic development for deserving youth, enabling them to become responsible leaders and contributors to society.

Vision

A community where every deserving youth is empowered through education to achieve their full potential and create positive impact.

Our Journey

For more than 35 years, SMCFI has continuously expanded its reach and impact. From a small initiative supporting a handful of scholars, it has grown into a foundation that has supported hundreds of graduates and thousands of students.

Through the years, the organization has built strong partnerships with alumni, corporate sponsors, and community stakeholders to sustain and expand its mission.
